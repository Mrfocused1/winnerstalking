About the Hosts

Winners Talking Podcast is a UK-based show hosted by Pound Sterling and Big Doug. From the streets of South East London, they bring raw, unfiltered conversations covering everything from social issues and current affairs to music, sport, and culture. With a roster of guests spanning the worlds of grime, drill, football, business, and beyond, every episode delivers real talk, big laughs, and perspectives you won't hear anywhere else.

Pound Sterling

Rapper from the UK hip-hop scene. His track "For My Dogs" dominated Channel U charts for ten weeks, and "So Real" hit #1. A business-minded entrepreneur who brings sharp insight and energy to every episode.

Big Doug

South East London's own. Full-time railway career by day, co-host of a MOBO-nominated podcast, SE Dons squad member, founder of Lean Gang UK clothing, and events promoter behind Lunare Nights. A man juggling multiple responsibilities at the highest level.
